The MotiBot application described is solely intended for research purposes. It has been developed by a team of Computer Science students from Laguna State Polytechnic University (LSPU) San Pablo as part of their capstone project. MotiBot aims to test the suitability of the Bi-directional Long Short-Term Memory (BiLSTM) algorithm for potential applications in mental health support. Please note that while the students have applied rigorous research and cutting-edge technologies in its development, MotiBot has not undergone validation for practical use. Users should be aware that its effectiveness as a mental health support tool has not been confirmed, and it should not be relied upon as such.
